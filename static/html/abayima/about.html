about abayima

Abayima is a non-profit (501c3 status pending) organization that works to support open access to information for citizens in North and Sub-Saharan Africa. Our mission is to support the advancement of access to technology for the poor.

We're an NGO working in a number of areas: human rights and disaster response, access to information, open governance and news dissemination.
