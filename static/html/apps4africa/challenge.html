Apps4Africa Challenges

There are two ways to be accepted into the Apps4Africa Acceleration program, through our Challenges or as an Entrepreneur-In-Residence program.

Challenges are coordinated with fiscally sponsoring partners who work with us to shape a competitive funding opportunities for companies that meet specific criteria. This allows them to find the companies that they are best suited to be able to help, while also offering an opportunity for the companies to raise seed capital. The winners of these Challenges are offered participation in the Accelerator as a complimentary part of their prize package.

To date we've facilitated the following Challenges:

2010 CIVIC CHALLENGE
Primary Partner: U.S. Department of State
Description: Focus was on solutions that created a better environment for civil society. 3 participants received $25,000 in funding

2011 CLIMATE CHALLENGE
Primary Partner: U.S. Department of State
Description: Focus was on solutions that addressed climate adaptation and agriculture. 9 participants received $75,000 in funding

2012 BUSINESS CHALLENGE
Primary Partners: U.S. Department of State, World Bank
Other Partners: LIONS@Frica, Microsoft, DEMO Africa, Hive Colab, AfriLabs
Description: Focus was on companies that were business centric and solutions that supported business. 3 participants received $30,000 in funding
