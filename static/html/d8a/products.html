THE D8A STACK

Data Driven Solutions for Human Problems

D8A Group [formerly MetaLayer] makes products that transform Twitter and other social data streams into actionable insights, helping clients in public safety, crime prevention, and finance make more informed, critical real-time decisions.

Our suite of predictive analytic products lets users see patterns in online behavior and match it to offline events. Our proprietary algorithms instantly scan hundreds of millions of online activities to provide the earliest alerts about what's happening (and what may happen soon). This helps users anticipate where, when, and what people need in real-time.
