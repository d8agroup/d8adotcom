About D8A

D8A (pronounced "dee" "ayht" "aye") is an award winning design consultancy based out of Philadelphia, PA. Our expertise is delivering technical solutions, data informed design, and consultation around client strategy and communications.

D8A is triple entendre, of sorts. It can be pronounced as individual letters, or as 'daytah' to represent our passion for data science and open data. But less obvious is the underlying meaning to our team where D8A refers to the eight letters in the word 'diaspora', paying homage to our companies origins in Africa.

Our Story

The beginnings of D8A go back to 2006 when CEO Jon Gosier freelanced for a number of leading media, film and advertising agencies. He soon took those experiences international, doing for some of the world's leading technology and media companies. Our love for design, visualization, and global markets eventually brought us right back home to the U.S. where D8A was established.
