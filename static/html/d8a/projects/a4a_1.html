    Supporting African Innovation and Business Development

    Apps4Africa is the longest running, highly successful impact investment competition on the African continent. The program offers a unique opportunity for African technologists to compete for startup funding and to participate in the Apps4Africa Acceleration program designed to help business develop succeed.

    One of our implementing partners over the past few years has been the U.S. Department of State’s Bureau of African Affairs. We developed and have maintained Apps4Africa’s online presence and community via social media channels over the last 4 years.

    To date, the program has funded 15 entrepreneurs from 12 African countries with over $130,000. To learn more, visit Apps4Africa.org

    The continued success of this project highlights our teams ability to craft global engagement strategies for organizations looking to make social impact.

    This multi-country program that has attracted global sponsors and international press is an example of our teams ability to help organizations not only design effective strategies but its also an example of our ability to help implement strategies.
