    Visualizing Real-Time Global to TED Talks

    At TED 2012 D8A unveiled a project that monitored, in real-time, the reaction across social media channels. Working with our partners at TED, D8A designed and built a fully interactive, 3D data-visualization depicting the global excitement for the TED conference.

    This visualization was shown throughout the conference and all attendees were encouraged to interact with the results.
