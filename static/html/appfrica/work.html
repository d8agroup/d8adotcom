Our Work in Africa

Appfrica is a social enterprise who's mission is to accelerate the growth of Africa's technology sector. To accomplish this, we carry out what some might consider to be an impressive array of project across Africa, working with both partners and on our own.

Here are some examples of our work:

Africa's Entrepreneur Accelerator

Apps4Africa is an acceleration program we founded to help discover and fund Africa's next generation of brilliant innovators.

Innovation Hub

We founded, Hive CoLab, Uganda's own innovation hub and collaborative workspace in the capital of Kampala.

Software Development

We've developed software solutions for partners like Frontline:SMS, Grameen Foundation, UNICEF, LivingGoods, and Google.

Promoting STEM Education

We had the pleasure of participating in British Airway's flagship UnGrounded "hackathon" in the sky" to promote STEM education.

Crisis Response and Human Rights

We've developed and maintain a number of efforts for protecting human rights.

Localization

In 2008 we helped Google translate their website to make it relevant in Uganda.

Data-Visualization

We help our partners make sense of large amounts of data through visualization.

Open Data

Recently we launched a portal for sharing African data and research.
